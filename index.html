What is this tool?

This is a tool that helps you evaluate how you feel about the possibility of acheiving your goals.

Instructions

  • Press the orange button to create a new slider.
  • Write the name of the goal you want to achieve.
  • Move the slider in the direction that you feel is correct for how you feel about the possibility of acheiving your goal
